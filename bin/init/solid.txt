SOLID PRINCIPLES


They are code principles that guard or guide the development and maintainability of code across multiple platforms and users specification.

Advantages
1. Extensibility: Can be easily extended
2. Maintainability
3. Easily Managed

WHAT ARE THE PRINCIPLES

S - SINGLE RESPONSIBILITY
O - OPEN CLOSED
L - LISKOV SUBSTITUTION
I - INTERFACE SEGREGATION
D - DEPENDENCY INJECTION
